Kalops

About

The Kaops dish is a traditional Swedish stew made of beef. The prominent flavours are Allspice and bayleafes.

Ingredients

Serves 4

  • 1 kg whole chuch of beef
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • 2 table spoons plain flour
  • 1 liter calf broth
  • 20 whole allspice seeds
  • 5 bayleafes
  • 4 Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the chuch into 3cm cubes
  2. Heat up a pot to high
  3. Fry the cubes on all size until they have a good sear
  4. Chop the onion into medium size bits
  5. Add the copped onions to the meat and fry for a couple of minutes
  6. Dust flour over the meat and stir to incorporate
  7. Add broth until it covers
  8. Add Bayleafes and allspice
  9. Stir and bring to a boil. Let simmer under lid for 75 minutes. Stir the pot occationally
  10. Cut carrots into smaller pieces
  11. Add carrots to the stew
  12. Peal and boil potatoes
  13. When potatoes are done so are the stew
    14. Enjoy!
