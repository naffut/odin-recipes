Meatballs in tomatosause

About

This version of the dish is invented by me in australia(Probably not in a unique way). I was cooking it for my girlfriend and stated that "in swedish this is translated to moose-poop".

Which it ofcourse isn't, but in my home this dish now goes under the name moose-poop.

Ingredients

Serves 4

  • 800g of mince beef
  • 4 table spoons of breadcrumbs
  • splash of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 medium onions
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • italian spice mix
  • slat and pepper
  • olive oil
  • two cans of chopped tomatoes
  • pasta of your choice

Steps

  1. In a bowl add crumbs and milk and stir, let rest for a couple of minutes
  2. Finly chop onions and garlic
  3. Add beef to bowl and mix well
  4. Add egg to bowl and mix
  5. Form balls from meatmixture, about 3cm in diameter
  6. Add olive oil to a hot pan
  7. Dust flour over the meat and stir to incorporate
  8. Add broth until it covers
  9. Add Bayleafes and allspice
  10. Stir and bring to a boil. Let simmer under lid for 75 minutes. Stir the pot occationally
  11. Cut carrots into smaller pieces
  12. Add carrots to the stew
  13. Peal and boil potatoes
  14. When potatoes are done so are the stew
    15. Enjoy!
